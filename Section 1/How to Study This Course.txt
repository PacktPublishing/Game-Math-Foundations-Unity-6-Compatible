How to Study This Course
This course contains both theory and practical elements.  The practical elements are designed for you to follow along with the instructor.

Throughout you will also find challenges posed in the videos.  You will be required to pause the video and complete the challenge before continuing.

As this is a mathematics course there are many formulae you will encounter along the way.  For your own benefit it would be a good idea to take notes during the videos and screenshot or write down any formulas so you can refer to them later as needed.
